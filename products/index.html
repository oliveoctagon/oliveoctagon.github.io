PRODUCTS

Excel-centric XBRL reporting software

Olive Octagon XBRL reporting products are all "Excel-centric". What do we mean by this? Well, all you need is Excel (either installed or in-browser) in order to complete the templates, and everything else is done through your web browser.

Our browser-based processing approach allows market-leading data security. When you access our software, all of the code required to validate and convert the completed Excel templates into XBRL (whether XBRL-XML or XBRL-CSV) is sent to your browser and is executed there. None of your data ever leaves the browser, nothing is uploaded to our servers, and browser-based validation and XBRL conversion means there's no software to install. As long as you can access our website and have a reasonably up-to-date browser (sorry, but the Internet Explorer days are gone!) then you can meet your reporting obligations with Olive Octagon.

We are always adding to our range of available products, including different XBRL reporting taxonomies.

At Olive Octagon our primary goal is to make the end product as user-friendly as possible. Therefore, we need to invest some effort in development and testing the first time we implement a particular XBRL taxonomy.
